Who I Am

I'm Osias Alcover Nalaunan III, a Bachelor of Science in Customs Administration (BSCA) student at Aklan State University, New Washington Campus — and I call myself a Vibe Coder.

I build practical, free web tools that solve real problems in Philippine trade and customs. Every project starts from a genuine need I see in my coursework or community.

What makes my setup unique: I develop entirely on an Android device (Vivo Y04) using Termux, ACode, and GitHub — no laptop, no desktop. Just pure grind.

My goal is to create free alternatives to expensive software so classmates and Filipino customs professionals can access the tools they need without barriers.
